# Experimentation pitfalls in product analytics

This draft covers common causes of weak experiment conclusions in analytics programs.

## Frequent issues

- Mismatch between metric and hypothesis.
- Underpowered samples interpreted as negative results.
- Multiple simultaneous changes with unclear attribution.
